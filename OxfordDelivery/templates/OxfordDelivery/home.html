ABOUT OX-D

Oxford Deliveries is a registered small scale company that is committed to providing fast and reliable delivery services. In just three weeks, we have already served over ten valued customers with over 22 successful deliveries. Our mode of transportation is the eco-friendly and sustainable bicycle, which allows us to offer quick and efficient delivery services to our customers. At Oxford Deliveries, we pride ourselves on delivering everything and anything, from baked goods and cold drinks to groceries from Shoprite as well as bevrages from Peter's Resturant. We understand the struggles faced by elderly people in our community, and we have made it our mission to provide a solution to this problem. Our goal is to make life easier for our customers, and we do so by delivering anything they need right to their doorstep. Our diverse range of offerings includes everything from airtime and candy from local tuckshops to confidential products from pharmacies. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service, and we are always looking for new and innovative ways to improve our delivery process. In conclusion, Oxford Deliveries is more than just a delivery company, we are a family committed to serving the people of Lenasia South with the utmost care and professionalism. Our motto is "Delivering Happiness, One Bicycle at a Time." Join the Oxford Deliveries family today, and experience the difference for yourself.
